
Video_symplex_evolution_and_values: launches the video showing the evolution of the symplexes at each iteration, along with the corresponding graphs indicating the value of the best point in the symplex at each step.

Funz_video_nelder and Nelder_Mead_stampa_valori are the functions used by Video_symplex_evolution_and_values in order to operate properly.
